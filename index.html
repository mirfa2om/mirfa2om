Welcome to Mirfa 2 RO Water Desalination Company LLC

Who We Are

Mirfa 2 RO Water Desalination Company LLC is responsible for the long-term operation and maintenance of the Mirfa 2 Reverse Osmosis Desalination Plant located in Al Dhafra Region, Abu Dhabi. We are committed to operational excellence, asset reliability, and water sustainability.

Safety and environmental responsibility are core principles of our operational culture. We adhere to the highest health, safety, and environmental (HSE) standards to protect our employees, contractors, community, and the environment. Our goal is zero harm — to people and to the planet — through a proactive safety culture and environmentally conscious practices across all levels of plant operation.

What We Do

Operating under a 30-year O&M Agreement, our company ensures safe, cost-efficient, and continuous production of 120 million imperial gallons per day (MIGD) of potable water, which is equivalent to approximately 545,500 cubic meters per day (m³/d).

To give you a sense of scale, 545,500 m³ of water per day could fill approximately 218 Olympic-sized swimming pools every 24 hours. Imagine that amount spread over a football field—it would reach a depth of nearly 75 meters!

Per hour, the plant produces over 22,700 cubic meters of potable water—enough to meet the average daily water consumption of more than 150,000 people in the GCC region. That’s equivalent to filling one Olympic pool every 6.6 minutes, or about 9 water tankers (12,000 liters each) every minute.

This output supports not just households, but also critical infrastructure, agriculture, and industry in the UAE. It reflects the region’s strategic investments in sustainable desalination technologies to secure water resilience for generations to come.
